OREO COOKIE ICE CREAM

Description

Oreco cookie ice cream is the best of all time

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) package chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • ½ (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Crush 20 Oreo® cookies. Reserve remaining cookies for another use
  2. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in crushed Oreo's®, condensed milk, sugar, and vanilla without destroying the fluff of the whipped cream.
  3. Transfer into a freezer-safe container and freeze until firm, at least 4 hours.
